New prime grade A office accommodation with outstanding green credentials in Edinburgh's thriving Exchange District.

Edinburgh's newest office development boasts outstanding green credentials, great facilities and bright, flexible and adaptable spaces.  The redevelopment of 30 Semple Street provides exceptional modern office accommodation with the highest environmental and sustainability accreditations in Scotland.

Buildings are too often completely demolished. By stripping back to the original frame, 30 Semple Street has saved 3,300 tons of CO2, more than 50% of the carbon that would have been used in reconstruction. All new class-leading energy efficient systems, stunning contemporary facades, crisp aesthetics and amenities of the highest quality.
